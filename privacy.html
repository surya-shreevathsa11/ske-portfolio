Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Sri Kaveri Electricals ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at srikaverielectricals.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website or contact us about our electrical design and implementation services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with us:

  • Contact details you provide voluntarily, such as your name, phone number, email address, and project location.
  • Project information you share with us, including site details, drawings, specifications, and enquiry messages sent via email or WhatsApp.
  • Technical data collected automatically by your browser or device, such as IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited. We do not currently use analytics or advertising trackers on this website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide quotes for our services.
  • Plan, design, supply, install, and commission electrical systems as agreed with you.
  • Communicate with you about project scope, scheduling, and documentation.
  • Maintain business records required for accounting, compliance, and warranty purposes.
  • Improve our website and service delivery.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on your consent when you contact us, our legitimate business interests in responding to enquiries and delivering services, and our legal obligations where applicable under Indian law.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary to deliver our services, such as with subcontractors, suppliers, or consultants involved in your project, or when required by law, court order, or government authority.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may include embedded content or links to third-party services, including:

  • Google Maps for location display. Google may collect data according to its own privacy policy.
  • WhatsApp when you choose to contact us through a WhatsApp link. Meta/WhatsApp's terms and privacy policy apply to messages sent through that platform.
  • Google Fonts for typography delivery.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party platforms. We encourage you to review their policies before using those services.

6. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and project-related information for as long as needed to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, including any warranty, tax, or regulatory retention requirements. When information is no longer required, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymise it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us at admin@srikaverielectricals.com.

8. Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised version will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
