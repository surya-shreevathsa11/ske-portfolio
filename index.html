How We Work

Our Process

From first site visit to final handover: a structured, phased approach to every electrical project.

01 01 / Understand

Scope the Site

A site visit or detailed brief: we understand the building, load requirements, and existing conditions before anything is drawn.

02 02 / Design

Draw the System

Our team drafts a complete electrical layout: load schedules, circuit drawings, and panel specifications, ready for approval.

03 03 / Plan

Approve & Source

Once the design is confirmed, we plan the implementation phase: sourcing quality materials and scheduling the installation team.

04 04 / Execute

Build & Hand Over

We complete the full installation to specification, test every circuit, and hand over with complete documentation and a walkthrough.
