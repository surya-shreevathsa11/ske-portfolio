HOW WE WORK

Our Process

01

Understand the Scope

We begin with a site visit or detailed brief to understand the scale, requirements, and existing conditions of your project.

02

Design the System

Our team drafts a complete electrical layout — load schedules, circuit drawings, and panel specifications tailored to your building.

03

Approve & Plan

Once the design is confirmed — ours or yours — we plan the implementation phase, handling sourcing and scheduling. For third-party work, this is where we begin.

04

Execute & Hand Over

We complete the installation to specification and hand over full documentation with a thorough walkthrough — leaving the site clean and ready.
