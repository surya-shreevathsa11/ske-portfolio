About Sri Kaveri Electricals.

Sri Kaveri Electricals is a professional electrical services firm based in Bengaluru, Karnataka. For over a decade, we have delivered complete electrical systems for residential complexes, commercial buildings, and industrial facilities across the city and region.

We work across all phases — design, supply, installation, and commissioning — with a commitment to BIS-compliant standards and transparent execution.

How We Work

Our Process

01 / Understand

Scope the Site

A site visit or detailed brief — we understand the building, load requirements, and existing conditions before anything is drawn.

02 / Design

Draw the System

Our team drafts a complete electrical layout — load schedules, circuit drawings, and panel specifications, ready for approval.

03 / Plan

Approve & Source

Once the design is confirmed, we plan the implementation phase — sourcing quality materials and scheduling the installation team.

04 / Execute

Build & Hand Over

We complete the full installation to specification, test every circuit, and hand over with complete documentation and a walkthrough.
